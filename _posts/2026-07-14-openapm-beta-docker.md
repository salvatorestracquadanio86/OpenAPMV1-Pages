The OpenAPM Docker image is available.

**OAH** and **WOM** — the first two modules of the system — can be installed locally with a single command. MongoDB is included in the stack, no external dependencies.

Full installation instructions: [Install OpenAPM]({{ '/install/' | relative_url }})

---

One important note before you start: this is a beta, and as such it reflects the current state of development. OAH and WOM work, but the system is still missing modules that would make it truly complete for day-to-day operational use.

For this reason, I recommend loading the demo data included in the package — it adds context, shows the flows between modules, and makes the evaluation far more meaningful than starting from an empty system.

---

During this period I will be collecting bugs — there will be some, it is a beta — and feedback from anyone who tries the system. Everything that comes up will feed into **beta 2**, which will also bring the **INS module** (Inspection Module): structured inspections, NDT campaigns, safety and compliance audits.
