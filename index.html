The Three Pillars of OpenAPM
The Problem

Large organizations have extraordinary tools.
What about SMEs?

Over the last 10 years working in Asset Performance Management, I've witnessed a consistent pattern: highly sophisticated solutions exist for large organizations, but small and medium-sized enterprises face the same challenges without the same access to tools.

The underlying need is identical — understand assets, preserve their value, manage them in a structured and informed way. The tools, however, remain out of reach.

📋

Untracked Maintenance

Maintenance activities are difficult to track, managed through spreadsheets and informal procedures.

🧠

Knowledge in a Few Minds

Critical operational knowledge is concentrated in individuals, creating fragility and single points of failure.

📁

Scattered Documentation

Documentation lives across folders, emails, and spreadsheets — never centralized, never searchable.

⚙️

Invisible Processes

Processes exist in practice but not in a structured system — impossible to audit, improve, or scale.

The Vision

What does "Open" really mean?

The "Open" in OpenAPM is not about open source software. It means a platform genuinely open to the widest possible range of organizations.

🏭

Open to Complex Organizations

For enterprises with complex operational structures that need a flexible foundation on which to build tailored APM processes — without the constraints of rigid off-the-shelf tools.

🏢

Open to SMEs

For organizations that want to introduce APM practices but lack the financial or organizational resources required for large-scale enterprise projects.

🤝

Complementary, Not Competitive

OpenAPM is not designed to compete with major industry players. It explores whether there is room for a solution that makes APM concepts more accessible and gradual to adopt.

Core Principles

How OpenAPM is different

Four fundamental principles guide every decision in the development of OpenAPM.

🎯

Simplicity First

No APM specialization required. Guided workflows and templates make best practices accessible to every operational team.

🤖

AI-Powered Guidance

AI assistance helps users understand not just what to do, but why — bridging the gap between industry knowledge and everyday operations.

🌐

Gradual Adoption

Start with what you need, grow at your own pace. No big-bang implementation, no massive upfront investment or organizational disruption.

💡

Free to Use

The majority of functionality will be available free of charge, allowing professionals and organizations to experiment and provide feedback.
